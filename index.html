Malumfashi Development Association(MADA)

Malumfashi Development Association is a group created only
for the indigenous persons of malumfashi and it's environs.
It's a group of selected responsible individuals who have
attain sucesses in their various fields of endeavours.
It's sole purpose is to mutually share knowledge
and ideas with a view to promote the cause of
economic development of malumfashi and it's environs.

MISSION

What is our mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to remain true to our community by providing the best economic oppurtunites for each and every member of our society.

VISION

What is our vision

Our vision

Our vision is to be the most socio-economic association of malumfashi community
